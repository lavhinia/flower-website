Our Story

Floralnia was founded in 2024 by Lavhinia Joice Basilio, a passionate florist and huge fan of Taylor Swift. What began as a small home-based business quickly blossomed into a local flower shop known for creative designs and concepts.

Today, our team of skilled florists continues Lavhinia's vision of creating beautiful, meaningful floral experiences that bring joy to the community.

Our Values

At Floralnia Boutique, we believe in the power of flowers to convey emotions, mark special moments, and bring beauty to everyday life. Our core values guide everything we do:

Quality: We select only the freshest, premium blooms for our arrangements.
Creativity: We approach each design with artistic vision and attention to detail.
Sustainability: We prioritize eco-friendly practices and locally-sourced flowers when possible.
Community: We support local events and give back through floral donations to community organizations.
